Indian   Football   School   insights

Culture of Excellence

The Indian Football School is an elite football training institution aimed at developing grass root football across India. The year- round player development programs will kick off in Mumbai on 6th Jan 2013 across 2 centres, the historical Cooperage Football Grounds in Churchgate and the modern day centre of sporting excellence, the Andheri Sports Complex.

Inaugural  IFS Strength  &  Conditioning Clinic,  Goa  2014

Indian Football School kicked of its 2014 courses in style by arranging for a week long strength and conditioning clinic for 18 IFS students in April. The boys were thrilled to experience how it is to train like a professional with IFS head coach, Ex – India International Alex Ambrose in the hub of Indian football, Goa.

Oscar  Foundation  wins  the  IFS  Youth  Cup  2014

The winning team Oscar Foundation took home the prestigious IFS youth Cup. The boys from Oscar foundation fought hard against the boys from Pune FC in the final, but ultimately kept their cool and were crowned champions of the IFS Youth Cup 2014

IFS   cup   2013   tournament   completion

The much awaited inaugural U-14 IFS cup was held at the cooperage football grounds on 11th & 12th Dec’13. The games were held at the world class Astro Turf pitches of the Indian Football School which saw 16 teams battle for the coveted IFS cup along with generous cash prizes, medals, certificates and prizes.
