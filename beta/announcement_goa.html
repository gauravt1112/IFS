IFS   announces   strength   and   conditioning   clinic,   Goa   2014

Indian Football School is proud to launch its much awaited inaugural Fitness & Conditioning Clinic in Goa for the IFS players in the U/17 category. The clinic will be driven by IFS Lead Coach Alex Ambrose in a fun filled yet physically intensive week comprising of football training, workshops and matches in Goa.

The clinic will continue to preach the philosophy of IFS which is to encourage the students to train, learn and believe. The boys will be exposed to playing and training as a team away from home, competing in matches against new opponents and experiencing different playing conditions.
