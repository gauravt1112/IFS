Indian Football School (IFS) is proud to announce Mumbai’s premier 5 a side football league on the world class artificial turf pitches at the historical Cooperage Football Grounds, in Churchgate, Mumbai. The league will host teams all across Mumbai who will battle it out to be crowned the kings of Mumbai!

Teams will battle it out in a fun and exciting league format leading to the knockout stages where the ultimate champion will be decided.

With attractive cash prizes and complimentary playtime on the world class pitches at the cooperage on offer this is a must for football fanatics all over Mumbai.
