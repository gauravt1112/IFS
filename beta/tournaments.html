IFS   Tournaments

The Indian Football School along with its established coaching programs also regularly hosts tournaments for football enthusiasts across its world class artificial turf pitches in Mumbai. With the recently concluded IFS CUP 2013 we look forward to our next tournament the IFS Youth Cup for the U-12 category in January 2104.

For more information on the IFS Youth Cup 2014 please call on - +912222844545

Teams choosing to participate in the same will have the choice to play in either of our 2 centres, in Churchgate or Andheri

IFS   Tournaments   Features

  • Matches on all weather astro turf
  • 6 v 6 / 5 v 5 format
  • Round robin & Knockout
  • Trophies & Medals for winners and runners up
  • Participation certificates
  • Attractive cash prizes for winning teams
  • Tournaments star player & goal keeper recognition
