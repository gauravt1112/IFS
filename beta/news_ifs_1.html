Congratulations  to  Oscar  Foundation  on  winning  the  IFS  Youth  Cup  2014

The much awaited inaugural IFS U-12 Youth Cup took place at the prestigious cooperage grounds on 18th and 19th Jan 2014. The tournament hosted 16 teams which included teams not only from Mumbai but a team from Pune (Pune FC) as well. The teams battled it out first in the group stages leading up to knockout stages and the grand final.

The winning team Oscar Foundation took home the prestigious IFS youth Cup. The boys from Oscar foundation fought hard against the boys from Pune FC in the final, but ultimately kept their cool and were crowned champions of the IFS Youth Cup 2014.

Indian Football School was honoured to host the tournament and is looking forward  to more such fun and thrilling events in the future.
