Indian   Football   School   announces   Invitational   Gala   2014   for   schools   @   Andheri   Sports   Complex

The IFS gala is a one of its kind 3 day festival for 8 lucky schools who will be invited to partake in fun and exciting programs organized keenly by the IFS team. Over the 3 days students will “Experience the Difference” with IFS where they will partake in exclusive football training sessions delivered by the esteemed IFS coaches keeping the institutions philosophy in mind which is to learn, train & believe. This will be followed by a tournament where 8 teams will be gunning for the coveted IFS Invitational Cup 2014.

During the given time slot football training sessions will be provided by the IFS coaching team to children from the respective school. While coaching is being conducted an induction program will be provided to the parents by the IFS staff.

The induction will talk about the world class IFS training programs and work carried out with partners such as the Western Indian Football Association. Inputs and thoughts will also be shared on the future of football in India, the role IFS hopes to play in this regard and advice on the professional pathways available for the student.

The taster session will be followed by the IFS Invitational Cup where the players will participate in a fun and exciting tournament on world class FIFA sanctioned artificial turf.
