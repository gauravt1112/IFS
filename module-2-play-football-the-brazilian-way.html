Module  2  –  Play  Football  the  Brazilian  Way!

Play like Ronaldo, Bebeto & Romario

The Indian Football School in association with youth development partners- Mumbai FC offer you a chance to train with the elite on the best facility this city has to offer!

World class turfs & only the best coaches from Brazil Module 2 will focus on techniques, tactics and strategies that players utilize in an attacking situation. Brazilian players, over generations have mastered the art of dominating in 1x2, 2x3 situations exploiting defense’s regularly, an art we hope to impart onto our students amongst numerous other exciting techniques. Legends such as Ronaldo, Bebeto and Romario will forever remain as the greatest attacking players in world football and we are privileged to have the best coaches from Brazil here in Mumbai to impart the same techniques to our students.
