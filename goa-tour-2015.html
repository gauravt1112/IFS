Announcements

GOA  TOUR  2015

Indian Football School is proud to launch its much awaited Fitness & Conditioning Clinic in Goa. The clinic will be driven by IFS – BFA (Brasil Futebol Academia) lead coaches in a fun filled yet physically intensive week comprising of football training, workshops and matches in Goa.

The clinic will continue to preach the philosophy of IFS which is to encourage the students to train, learn and believe. The boys will be exposed to playing and training as a team away from home, competing in matches against new opponents and experiencing different playing conditions.

To Register Call us on – +918080297427; email at Sajid@indianfs.in

Key Program Features

  • Intense IFS fitness and conditioning sessions @ Colva beach
  • IFS – BFA training sessions on world class training facilities in Goa
  • Training sessions conducted by Ex-Indian National Team Footballers and Coaching Staff
  • Workshops by Ex-Indian National Team Footballers And Coaching Staff
  • 3 Matches in the prestigious Quepem Football Stadium
  • Conditioning sessions in swimming pool
  • Leisure / sightseeing visits organized during free time

Logistics Details

  • Mode Of Transport – By Air
  • Internal transport will be arranged
  • Accommodation Included - @ Sincoro Hotel, Colva
  • Breakfast /Lunch / Dinner / Snacks will be provided
  • All costs included in the clinic fee
  • Leisure trip cost included
