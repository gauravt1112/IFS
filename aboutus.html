about   Us

Indian Football School &   Brasil Futebol Academia

The Indian Football School is a premier football training institution aimed at providing the Best Football Training Programs in Mumbai and developing grass root football across India. The year round football training programs are delivered across 2 centers, the historical Cooperage Football Grounds in Churchgate and the modern day centre of sporting excellence, Andheri Sports Complex. Both our centers are equipped with the following:

  • World Class FIFA sanctioned astro turf facilities
  • State Of the art player rooms equipped with lockers and showers
  • Lounge area for parents and guardians
  • Class Rooms for health, nutrition and lifestyle workshops
  • Fully equipped Physio rooms

The Indian Football Schools is managed by the same team and coaching staff that successfully ran the Manchester United Soccer Schools programs across India for over 2 years. Our core coaching staff are committed to deliver top quality player development programs to the students across our world class training facilities where we will build on our effort of last 2 years.

Young footballers in Mumbai can now learn to play the Brazilian way, thanks to the technical partnership that the Indian Football School (IFS) is entering into with the Brasil Futbol Academia (BFA).

Indian Football School, which run two centers in Mumbai—at the Cooperage and Andheri Sports Complex will now gain from their association with BFA, who will add technical expertise and structure to the IFS program with the long term vision of developing young footballers enrolled into IFS courses.

IFS trainees will be given the opportunity to provide football coaching for kids in the Samba style of play under the BFA curriculum with learning’s introduced from the successful Brazilian philosophy. BFA will assign a Technical Director inMumbai, who will be in charge of all technical programming, progression plans and coaches.

The BFA-IFS tie-up will see the involvement of the likes of former star players Roberto Mendes da Silva (Beto), Jose Ramirez Barretto and Technical Director Glauco Moraes, who will be actively involved in the day to day running of the program.

IFS are also proud to announce its association with the esteemed Mumbai FC from the I League where IFS will support the ongoing youth development of Mumbai FC over the next many years. This association allows deserving IFS students to be regularly inducted into the esteemed Mumbai FC U-15, U-17 & U-19 teams, which will partake in numerous high profile tournaments most notable being the I-league!
