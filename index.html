Indian   Football   School   insights

Culture of Excellence

The Indian Football School is an elite football training institution aimed at developing grass root football across India. The year- round player development programs are delivered at  the historical Cooperage Football Grounds in Churchgate and the modern day centre of sporting excellence, the Andheri Sports Complex.
