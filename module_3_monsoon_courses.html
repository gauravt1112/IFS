Module  3  –  Monsoon  Courses

The Indian Football School in association with youth development partners- Mumbai FC and Brasil Futebol Academia offer you a chance to train with the elite on the best facility this city has to offer!

At Indian Football School we focus on the overall development of our students so that they are winners not only on the pitch but out of it as well. All our courses will focus Primarily on Technical, Tactical, Pshycology and Physical aspects of the game.

The young stars (younger age category) of IFS will be exposed to the fundamentals of football (foundation development) along fun filled drills and small sided games. The 10/14 age category students along with foundation training will also be trained on decision making, positioning,offensive and defensive play, systemic marking and experience in all positions.

The 15yrs+ students will be made to work on their fitness, core strenghnig along with prime focus on technical fundamentals and specific position play . The boys will be put through intensive long and shor duration excercises to improve fitness and endurance. Focus will be to build on physical attributes such as speed, strength, skills, endurance and muscle strength. They will also be exposed to Small tactical games(1*1)(2*1),corners,Fouls,Set plays,counterattacking play,transistion of offence into deffence,tactical formations(4-3-3),(3-5-2).

With IFS launching its U8s, U10s, U13s, U15s, U17s & U19s in association with Mumbai FC, be ready to give your best and make it count!!!
