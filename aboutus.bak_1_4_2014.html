about   indian   football   school

Culture   of   Excellence

The Indian Football School is an elite football training institution aimed at developing grass root football across India. The year round player development programs will kick off in Mumbai on 6th Jan 2013 across 2 centres, the historical Cooperage Football Grounds in Churchgate and the modern day centre of sporting excellence, the Andheri Sports Complex. Both our centres are equipped with the following:

  • World Class FIFA sanctioned astro turf facilities
  • State Of the art player rooms equipped with lockers and showers
  • Lounge area for parents and guardians
  • Class Rooms for health, nutrition and lifestyle workshops
  • Fully equipped Physio rooms

The Indian Football Schools is managed by the same team and coaching staff that successfully ran the Manchester United Soccer Schools programs across India over the last 3 years. Our core coaching staff are committed to deliver top quality player development programs to the students across our world class training facilities where we will build on our effort of last 3 years. Our curriculum has been adapted based on inputs from students, parents and leading international coaches from England and Germany.

The football development programs will be delivered throughout the year by our highly trained and qualified coaching staff. Our focus is to ensure overall development across 5 areas, Tactical, Physical, Social, Technical and Psychological. We also aim to develop squads / teams to compete at city and state level across age groups to provide the much needed competitive to edge to our students. Working with the All India Football Association and Western India Football Association to provide professional level career guidance to students of Indian Football School is the key driving force behind our vision of playing a pivotal role in development of football in India.

All   our   courses   include

  • Induction Ceremonies at the start of the programs
  • Parent Consultation Evenings
  • Player Report Cards
  • Course Completion Certificates
  • Star Player Recognitions
  • Graduation Ceremonies on program completion
  • Professional Careers Guidance
